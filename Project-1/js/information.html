About Our Company

Our History

Red River Sash Weavers was established in 1832 by a cooperative of Métis artisans dedicated to preserving and promoting the traditional art of finger-weaving. Our founders recognized that the Ceinture Fléchée represented not merely a practical garment, but a vital expression of Métis culture and identity.

Our History

Our mission is to preserve the traditional art of Métis finger-weaving while providing practical, beautiful, and durable sashes to customers throughout the dominion and beyond. We are committed to fair wages for our artisans, sustainable sourcing of materials, and maintaining the cultural integrity of this important Métis tradition.

Every purchase supports Métis families and helps ensure that this remarkable craft will be passed to future generations. We take pride in our role as custodians of this heritage and work diligently to honor those who came before us.
